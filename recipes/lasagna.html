Description

Lasagna is a classic baked pasta dish typically made with layers of flat pasta sheets, a rich meat sauce (often ragù), a creamy béchamel sauce, and various cheeses like ricotta and mozzarella. It's a hearty and satisfying dish that's often served as a main course or a family-style meal.
