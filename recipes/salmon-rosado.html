Description

Pink salmon, also known as "humpy" or "humpback salmon," has a pale pink flesh with a mild, delicate flavor. Its texture is described as softer than other salmon varieties, with small flakes. Pink salmon is a good source of lean protein and omega-3 fatty acids.

Ingredients

  • Pink Salmon: The primary ingredient, providing protein and healthy fats.
  • Water: May be used to maintain moisture or in some cases, as part of a protective ice glaze.
  • Salt: Used for seasoning and preservation.
  • Spices: Onion powder, garlic powder, dill, and other spices can be added for flavor.
  • Oils: Canola oil, sunflower oil, or olive oil may be used in cooking or for flavoring.
  • Other Additives: Some processed salmon products may contain modified milk ingredients, cornstarch, or other additives to alter texture or flavor.
  • Sauces: Dill sauce, honey chipotle sauce, or garlic butter can be used to enhance the flavor of the salmon.
